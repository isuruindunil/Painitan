Our Heritage

Mastering local delicacies since 2011.

Painitan.com is an evolution of the beloved Painitan Ni Papito, originally established in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu. We've brought our authentic flavors and unwavering commitment to quality to Iligan City.

We specialize in dishes that comfort the soul. From rich, flavorful Lugaw topped generously, to our signature Arrozcaldo, crispy Lumpia, hearty Batchoy, and perfect Silog combos paired with freshly brewed local coffee. Every bowl and plate is a celebration of our culinary heritage.
